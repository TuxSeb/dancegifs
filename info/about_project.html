About the projet

My goals

I want to make this website a place to learn different dance styles by practicing, from beginner to advanced, for free

Here's my roadmap for the next releases to come:

  • Adding content for salsa, tango and Rock'n roll
  • Adding share button support for every gifs

Note: Maybe not up-to-date

Source code is available Here and you can track progress on Trello

I'm using classic HTML/CSS with Boostrap 4 and JQuery, hosted on GitHub Pages, but planning to switch on a dedicated (Apache, PHP, SQL...) solution.

About me

Who am I ?

My name is Sébastien, I'm a 21 years old French computer science student who started dancing a few years ago with tango.

It wasn't really my choice a the beginning, I had to pick a semester option at the university, and it was the last one remaining among hundreds choices of activities 😅

But I found out I liked that, and I wanted to learn something else, with a higher pace and less "robotics" moves, so I chose salsa on my second year of university, now im here, making this website for fun, combining both computer science skills and my new interest in salsa and dances.

I sincerely hope you enjoy this website, have a great time ! 😉
